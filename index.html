The Science Behind Alzheimer’s

Understanding the disease through mathematics and physics

Why Tau Protein Matters

Alzheimer’s disease is strongly associated with the abnormal aggregation and spread of tau protein across brain regions. This process disrupts neuronal communication and leads to progressive cognitive decline.

  • Tau misfolding and aggregation
  • Spatial propagation across the brain
  • Neuronal network disruption

We describe this phenomenon mathematically using reaction–diffusion partial differential equations.
